Introduction

The IndEcol Digital Lab Wiki, maintained by the Industrial Ecology Digital Lab, focuses on the digital infrastructure of the Industrial Ecology Programme. It provides a group wide place to gather information about databases and software tools used in Industrial Ecology research. You can also find a list of ongoing projects and links, tutorials and guidelines to developmental tools used in IndEcol research. If you are looking for a specific information, try the search button on the left. You can find a guide how to access this wiki on the go here.

Feel free to add or edit any content. Just click on the edit the page button and get started. The IndEcol wiki is based on Confluence, check it out for more details. If you need further help or have any comments please contact admin@indecol.no. Of course, you are always invited to visit us the the Industrial Ecology Digital Lab and talk to us directly.
