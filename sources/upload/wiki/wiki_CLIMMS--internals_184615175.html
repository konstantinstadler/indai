Availability
This fileserver is in a HA-cluster with disaster failover to another HA cluster so it will be very good availability
We do regular maintenance once a month at the most frequent, and in this hours you will see som NFS-stale but traffic will go back to normal in a few minutes.

Backup
As for backup, we will keep snapshots for 6 month at weekly schedule, daily for last 32 days and hourly for the past 36 hours
