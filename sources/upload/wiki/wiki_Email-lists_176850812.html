Created by Unknown User (radekl), last modified by Konstantin Stadler on 05.02.2021

Below, the most used email lists of indecol. A full overview of available lists can be found by clicking here.

  • indecolfaggruppe@indecol.ntnu.no - all IndEcol people in EPT (professors, researchers, along with the PhDs and PostDocs in their groups)

  • professors@indecol.ntnu.no - Professors, Group Leaders and internal Admin of the IndEcol group

  • forum@indecol.ntnu.no - all IndEcol Professors, PhDs, PostDocs, and external people associated with IndEcol.

  • phd@indecol.ntnu.no - all PhDs and PostDocs in IndEcol.

Basic information on a list (in the domain indecol.ntnu.no) can be found here
https://lists.ansatt.ntnu.no/indecol.ntnu.no/info/EMAILLISTNAME
e.g.
https://lists.ansatt.ntnu.no/indecol.ntnu.no/info/indecolfaggruppe

Anne Lise Berg is managing the lists. Please contact her if you need to change membership of one of these or want to create a new one.
