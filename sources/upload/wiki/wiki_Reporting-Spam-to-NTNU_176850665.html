Industrial Ecology Digital Lab : Reporting Spam to NTNU

Created by Unknown User (radekl), last modified on 11.11.2019

You can help improving the Spam and suspicious e-mail detection algorithm of the NTNU email service.

If you receive any suspicious email, forward it as attachment(!!!) to soc@ntnu.no . In case you ask for receiving emails not filtered for Spam, forward any emails erroneously identified as Spam to dp-epost@it.ntnu.no .

Further details: https://innsida.ntnu.no/wiki/-/wiki/English/Report+suspicious+e-mail
