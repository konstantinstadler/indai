Industrial Ecology Digital Lab : Acknowledgment of the Digital Lab

Created by Konstantin Stadler, last modified on 13.02.2023


We would greatly appreciate it if the contributions of the Industrial Ecology Digital Lab were acknowledged in any manuscript (thesis, technical report, article, etc.) in which we assisted with code/data management and development, or in which the servers managed by us were utilized. This helps us track the scientific outcome of IndEcol investments in the Lab and encourages further investments.

Of course, we are still interested in co-authorship on articles where our contributions are on par with those of the other authors.

Please note that HPC providers IDUN (https://www.hpc.ntnu.no/idun/acknowledgment/) and Sigma2 (FRAM, BETZY, etc - https://www.sigma2.no/acknowledgements) have similar requirements.

If possible, please use the templates below for the acknowledgment (instead of acknowledging specific individuals at the digital lab), and feel free to adjust them based on our contributions. Also, please inform us as soon as the publication is released as we would like to compile a list of our contributions.
